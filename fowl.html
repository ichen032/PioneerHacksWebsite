Fowl

Reasons why YOU should get a fowl:

- they lay eggs
- their droppings can be used as fertilizer for your garden
- they have quirky personalities

corn, grains, vegetables, and insects

5-10 years for ducks and chickens
10-20 years for geese

1-2 years for quails

$200-400

outdoor area with at least 10 square feet per bird, complete with a coop for them to rest at night

- a backyard they can run around in; additionally a pond for the ducks and geese
- be aware of raccoons and stray cats that might threaten them

- these animals are very loud, so be prepared to hear bwak, quack, and honk all day
- these animals have wings, meaning that they might fly away at the first sign of danger

- these animals have chances of spreading diseases that are harmful to humans
